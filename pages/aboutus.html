Our Story

Float Audio was born out of a passion for high-quality sound and a desire to make premium audio experiences accessible to everyone. Founded in 2015 by a group of audio enthusiasts, we've grown from a small startup to a trusted name in the world of TWS earbuds and headphones.

At Float Audio, we're on a mission to revolutionize the way people experience sound. We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy music, podcasts, and calls with exceptional clarity and comfort. Our goal is to provide cutting-edge audio technology at prices that won't break the bank.
